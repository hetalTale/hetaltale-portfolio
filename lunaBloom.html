Luna Bloom

Luna Bloom is a self-created jewelry brand, inspired by the serene beauty of the moon and the organic forms of nature. Rooted in a desire to craft meaningful and enchanting pieces, the brand reflects a delicate balance between elegance and the natural world. For this project, I developed the brand name, designed the logo, and created visual mockups to bring the identity to life. Luna Bloom embodies a personal vision translated into a timeless aesthetic.
