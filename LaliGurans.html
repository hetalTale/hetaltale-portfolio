Lali Gurans

Lali Gurans is a menu design project for a fictional Nepali restaurant named after Nepal’s national flower, the rhododendron. The design reflects the warm, earthy, and vibrant essence of Nepali culture through thoughtful typography, layout, and color choices. Along with the menu, I created realistic mockups to showcase the brand’s cozy, authentic dining experience.
