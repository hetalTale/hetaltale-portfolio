Shine On Ad Campaign

For Tower 21’s Shine On lip gloss, I designed an imaginative ad campaign with mockups across social media and hoardings. The campaign explored bold, inclusive visuals to reflect the product’s universal appeal—showcasing how Shine On resonates with all ages, skin tones, and professions. This project combined storytelling, layout design, and brand-focused creativity to build a cohesive advertising identity.
