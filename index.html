About Me

I am a graphic designer and VFX artist who thrives in a world where boldness meets nostalgia. My design approach is rooted in maximalism, embracing intricate details, vibrant storytelling, and a seamless blend of old-school charm with modern energy. I have a deep love for bright, poppy colors that bring life to my work, reflecting my lively and expressive nature. My style balances playfulness and sophistication, creating visuals that are both striking and timeless.

I’m also a book lover, drawing inspiration from the stories, textures, and emotions found within pages. My passion for storytelling translates into my designs, where every element is carefully crafted to evoke a sense of depth and meaning. Beyond aesthetics, I’m a fun-loving, bubbly, and classy individual who finds joy in nature, textures, movement, and creativity. My curly-haired, expressive, and artistic spirit shines through in my work, ensuring each design is authentic, engaging, and unforgettable.

For me, design is more than just visuals, it’s about crafting experiences that evoke emotions and leave a lasting impression. Through bold compositions, rich textures, and thoughtful storytelling, I bring ideas to life in a way that is both dynamic and meaningful.
